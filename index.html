The Approach

Fewer engagements. Deeper work.

Sutton Strategies is a boutique by design. We represent a select group of advisors and firms at a time, so every transition gets the attention a life-changing decision deserves.

Discretion first

Exploring a move is sensitive. Conversations stay confidential from the first call through the day you're ready — if you're ever ready. No pressure, no noise.

Fit over placement

We map your practice, your clients, and your economics against the full landscape — wirehouse, regional, independent — and tell you the truth about each option, including staying put.

The long view

A transition isn't the finish line. We structure moves around where your business should be in ten years, not just the deal on the table today.
